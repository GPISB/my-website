ABOUT GP INTEGRASI

A Malaysian Engineering Contractor Established in 2019

GP Integrasi Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian engineering and contracting company providing Mechanical & Electrical Engineering, Fire Protection Systems, Building Maintenance and supporting Civil Works.

Our work covers the supply, installation, servicing, inspection, testing, commissioning, troubleshooting and maintenance of engineering systems for commercial, industrial, institutional and government-related facilities.

We approach every assignment with a focus on regulatory compliance, practical execution, quality workmanship and dependable support throughout the project lifecycle.
