About GP Integrasi

Built to deliver reliable engineering support with a focus on M&E and Fire Protection.

GP Integrasi Sdn. Bhd. is a Malaysian engineering company established to provide practical, responsive and safety-conscious solutions for commercial, industrial, institutional and government-related facilities.

Company Overview

A registered engineering company serving facility, safety and technical project needs.

GP Integrasi Sdn. Bhd. was incorporated in 2019 with the aim of providing dependable engineering services to clients requiring Mechanical & Electrical works, Fire Protection Systems, Building Maintenance and selected Civil Support Works.

Our primary strength is in M&E and fire protection. While civil works are part of our supporting capability, our main concentration remains on technical systems, fire safety, maintenance and engineering support.
